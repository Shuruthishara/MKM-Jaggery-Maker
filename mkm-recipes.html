Spread

Spread

Nattusakkarai when mixed with ghee, makes the best substitute for chutneys in dosa and idli for sweet lovers. This can also be spread on chapati and poori to make a sweeter and healthier tiffin for your kids.

Baking

Baking

To break the pattern of baking with conventional white sugar, we suggest you a healthier version. Bake any cake or cookie with nattu sarkarai by just increasing the amount of oils and butter by few tablespoons. You will find the taste more better enhanced and the dish, more healthier.

Banana Fritters

Banana Fritters

Commonly called pala kachayam or soyya maavu (because of the sound it makes when fried. This a easy and more healthy snack and savoury made by mashed bananas, and nattu sakkarai and all purpose flour and fried in small portions.

Hot Drink

Hot Drink

Boil dried coriander, dry ginger, a pinch of pepper in water and achu vellam in water till it dissolves. Add this decotion with hot milk to enjoy a healthier substitute for coffee and tea. Make sure you don't add jaggery to milk, it will curdle.

Panagam

Cold Drink

Dissolve jaggery in cold water and add a pinch of dried coriander powder, dry ginger powder, pepper and salt with few teaspoons of lemon juice. Have this drink on a hot sunny day to feel relaxing and cooled. It is a great substitute for toxic carbonated drinks.

Fruits Halwa

Fruits Halwa

Chop or grate any fruit of your choice and saute in ghee till it becomes mushy. Add nattusarkarai to it and mix till ghee separates. This can be best done with fruits like Jackfruit, carrot, beetroot, apple, chikkoo, pumpkin, ashgourd and nendran banana.
