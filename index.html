Achu Vellam

Achu vellam is the jaggery obtained from the syrup of sugar cane juice that is poured in molds. It is the unrefined and purest form of whole sugar.

Nutritional Facts

  • It serves as a natural source for various anti-oxidants, minerals and vitamins like Magnesium, Potassium, Iron, Zinc and Selenium.
  • Acts as a laxative.
  • Increases hemoglobin level in blood.
  • Boosts Immunity.
  • Prevents water retention.

100 g of Jaggery powder contains

Uses

Can be used as a healthy substitute for regular white sugar. A pinch of it can be added to traditional spicy food as a taste enhancer.

Urundai Vellam

Other names: Mandai Vellam, Paagu Vellam.
Urundai vellam is the sugar cane juice syrup that is made into large sized balls instead of being poured into molds. It is the best option for those who prefer a healthy substitute for sugar in large quantities.

Nutritional Facts

  • Improves digestion.
  • Purifies blood.
  • Free from artificial bleaching agents.
  • Improves metabolism, thereby reducing the ill effects of sweets.

100 g of Jaggery powder contains

Uses

Can be used for sweets, Pongal and beverages in large quantities.

Nattu Sarkarai

Other Name: Country sugar, Karumbu Sakkarai
Unlike the brown sugar made from molasses, the Nattu sakkarai is made by powdering the thickened sugar cane syrup.

Nutritional Facts

  • Rich in iron and Calcium.
  • Improves bone strength.
  • Helps against diabetes.
  • Improves skin and hair health.

100 g of Jaggery powder contains

Uses

It can be the best other way option for sugar in all drinks and confectionaries. It can be consumed by children and diabetics without any fear!

Brown Sugar*

Though brown in colour and resembles nattu sakkarai, it is made by mixing refined white sugar with the sugar cane molasses. They are partially refined sugar.

Nutritional Facts

  • Contains minerals like Iron, potassium and calcium.
  • It is a slightly better option to white sugar which has no nutrients.

Uses

Can be used in bakeries and industrial sweet manufacturing.
